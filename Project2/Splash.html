The Designer's Guild is an upcoming annual conference that is dedicated some of the most iconic and well known POP CULTURE Designers of our time. This four day event will include fun activities for future designers that includes Panels on how well do you know the logo and its Master of Design, what will the Pantone of 2024 be and the comeback of the Sans-Serif Typeface. From Logo, Fashion, Branding, and many other Designs, our conference will provide workshops for those who have a passion to go into this job field. Whether you're straight out of high school or fresh into college The Designer's Guild will help start your path into achieving your ulimate design goal!
