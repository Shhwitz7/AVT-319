Stacy Horowitz

Career: TORRID

Education: George Mason University

Location: Fairfax, VA

Stacy's Art Statement

Ms.Horowitz is currently residing in her hometown of Fairfax, Virginia, where she is also attending her senior year at George Mason Univeristy earning her BFA in Visuals Arts and Technology with a concentration in Graphic Design. Ms.Horowitz is also a part-time Sales Associate at the TORRID located at Fair Oaks Mall where she uses her skill and knowledge when it comes to all things Fashion and Design related. Ever since she was a little girl, Stacy has always been passionate for her love of art and drawing by expressing herself from her world visually. While attending Oakton High School, she was exoposed to the world of Graphic Design which better enhanced art skills both handered and digital. After graduating from GMU Stacy hopes to pursue her career by becoming a Concept Character Designer for Animation
